## Essence
2-week online intensive on the full SMM cycle — goal-setting & audience research → content production → Meta ad campaigns. 3 sessions/week online; 1 weekly office visit for content filming. By the end you can independently run a brand's social media and paid campaigns.

## Curriculum (6 modules from brochure)
1. **Dasar Social Media Marketing** — tujuan & sasaran SMM, analisis kompetitor, jenis promosi sosmed
2. **Content Strategy** — content plan, format konten, KPI sosmed, meningkatkan engagement
3. **Meta Business Tools** — Meta Business Suite, Ads Manager, katalog produk
4. **Targeting & Tracking** — audience parsing, pixel & retargeting, lookalike audiences, optimisasi audience
5. **Advertising Strategy** — pembuatan & optimisasi kampanye, analisis & pelaporan, bahasa konten promosi
6. **Lead Generation & Optimization** — target generation, plan & remarketing, analisis data & reporting kampanye
